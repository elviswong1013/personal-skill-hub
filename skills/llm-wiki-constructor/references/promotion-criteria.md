# Promotion Criteria

Consult this reference when deciding what to promote from source to higher layers (Step 6).

## Entity

Promote when: durable person, organization, tool, role, framework, book, or platform appearing across multiple source pages.

Do NOT promote from a single mention. An entity earns its page by recurrence.

## Concept

Promote when: stable, reusable idea bridging multiple entities or source pages with explanatory power beyond one context.

## Wiki

Promote when: the note is mainly useful for guided reading, comparison, or navigation.

Wiki pages organize existing entity and concept pages; they do not introduce new facts.
